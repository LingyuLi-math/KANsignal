## 2025.03.28
These gene list are download from https://housekeeping.unicamp.br/?download
- Housekeeping_GenesHuman.csv
	Housekeeping genes  管家基因	List of human genes stably expressed across 52 tissues and cells types.
	在 52 种组织和细胞类型中稳定表达的人类基因列表。
- Housekeeping_GenesMouse.csv
	Housekeeping genes  管家基因	List of mouse genes with at least one of their transcripts expressed across 14 tissues and cells types.
	至少一种转录本在 14 种组织和细胞类型中表达的小鼠基因列表。
- Human_Mouse_Common.csv
	Common genes  共同基因	This list shows the overlap of human and mouse housekeeping genes. 1130 genes were identified.
	此列表显示了人类和小鼠管家基因的重叠。已鉴定出 1130 个基因。
